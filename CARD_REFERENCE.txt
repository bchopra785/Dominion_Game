                            VICTORY CARDS (4)
                  Grant automation points at end of game
================================================================================

BUG
  Cost: 0
  Value: -1 point
  Category: Victory / Penalty
  Description: A penalty card that subtracts 1 automation point when the game 
               ends. Never playable in hand. Created when opponents play attack 
               cards (Hack, Evergreen Test, Ransomware).

METHOD
  Cost: 2
  Value: +1 automation point
  Category: Victory
  Description: Grants 1 automation point when the game ends. Base victory card.
               Purchased mid-game to start building end-game scoring.

MODULE
  Cost: 5
  Value: +3 automation points
  Category: Victory
  Description: Grants 3 automation points when the game ends. Higher-value 
               victory card for serious end-game strategy.

FRAMEWORK
  Cost: 8
  Value: +6 automation points
  Category: Victory
  Description: Grants 6 automation points when the game ends. The highest-value 
               card in the game. Primary end-game goal.


================================================================================
                        MONEY CARDS (3)
            Provide spendable currency during the MONEY phase
================================================================================

BITCOIN
  Cost: 0
  Value: 1 money
  Category: Money / Starter Card
  Description: Basic currency card included in starting hand. Free to acquire 
               from board. Grants 1 spendable money when played in MONEY phase.
               Foundation for all money production strategy.

ETHEREUM
  Cost: 3
  Value: 2 money
  Category: Money
  Description: Mid-tier currency card. Costs 3 money to acquire. Grants 2 
               spendable money per play. Better value than Bitcoin as you scale 
               your money production.

DOGECOIN
  Cost: 6
  Value: 3 money
  Category: Money / Premium
  Description: Premium currency card. Costs 6 money to acquire. Grants 3 
               spendable money per play. Highest money production per card but 
               expensive to buy.

--- BASIC ACTION CARDS ---

BACKLOG
  Cost: 2
  Effect: +1 Action
  Type: Cantrip (Action with no cost)
  Description: Discard any number of cards, then draw that many. Flexible card 
               cycling and deck management. Can help draw key cards or discard 
               bad cards like Bugs.

CODE_REVIEW
  Cost: 3
  Effect: +1 Card, +2 Actions
  Type: Cantrip Engine
  Description: Card draw and action acceleration. One of the strongest utility 
               cards for enabling action chains.

SPRINT_PLANNING
  Cost: 3
  Effect: +1 Card, +1 Action, +1 Buy
  Type: Advanced Cantrip
  Description: Balanced utility card combining card draw, extra action, and 
               shopping power. Cascades well with action chains. Enables buying 
               expensive cards.

UNIT_TEST
  Cost: 3
  Effect: Choose 1: +2 Actions OR +2 Money OR +2 Cards
  Type: Choice Card
  Description: Flexible choice card allowing strategic decision based on 
               current needs. Cantrip status means it doesn't consume an action.

--- MID-TIER ACTION CARDS ---

HACK
  Cost: 4
  Effect: +2 Money (Attack)
  Type: Attack Card
  Description: Attack card that also provides 2 money. Each other player 
               discards cards down to 3 cards in hand. COUNTERED by Monitoring.
               Generates opponent Bugs if not blocked.

TECH_DEBT
  Cost: 4
  Effect: +1 Card, +1 Action, +1 Money
  Type: Hybrid Card
  Description: Discard one card per empty Supply pile (board spaces where cards 
               are sold out). Scales with game state - becomes stronger as game 
               progresses and supplies empty.

REFACTOR
  Cost: 4
  Effect: Trash a card, gain one costing up to +2 more
  Type: Recycling / Engine Card
  Description: Core engine-building card. Trash (remove from game) a card, then 
               gain a card costing up to 2 more than the trashed card. Enables 
               deck quality improvement.

PARALLELIZATION
  Cost: 4
  Effect: Play an Action card from hand twice
  Type: Multiplier Card
  Description: Play an Action card from your hand twice without spending extra 
               action. Doubles the effect of powerful action cards. Strong 
               synergy card.

MERGE_CONFLICT
  Cost: 2
  Effect: Trash a card, draw cards equal to its cost
  Type: Scaling Card
  Description: Trash a card, then draw a number of cards equal to that card's 
               cost. Scaling effect - trashing expensive cards draws many cards.

MONITORING
  Cost: 2
  Effect: +2 Cards (Reaction Defense)
  Type: Reaction / Defense Card
  Description: Defensive reaction card that reveals during attack resolution to 
               negate attack effects. Stays in hand when used for defense. 
               Counters: Hack, Evergreen Test, Ransomware. Pure defense card.

--- PREMIUM ACTION CARDS ---

IPO
  Cost: 5
  Effect: +2 Cards, +1 Action, +2 Money
  Type: Premium Cantrip
  Description: One of the strongest cards in the game. Combines card draw (2), 
               action generation, AND money production. Enables playing multiple 
               expensive cards in one turn.

DEPLOYMENT_PIPELINE
  Cost: 5
  Effect: +1 Buy, +1 Money per empty Supply pile
  Type: Cost Reduction / Tempo
  Description: Provides an extra Buy and 1 money. Scales with game progression 
               as supplies empty. Enables shopping for multiple cards per turn.

EVERGREEN_TEST
  Cost: 5
  Effect: +2 Cards (Attack)
  Type: Attack Card
  Description: Attack card that also draws 2 cards. Each other player gains a 
               Bug to their discard pile. COUNTERED by Monitoring.

DAILY_SCRUM
  Cost: 5
  Effect: +4 Cards, +1 Buy
  Type: Premium Draw Card
  Description: Card draw assault - draw 4 cards and get an extra buy. Each other 
               player also draws a card (counterintuitively helps them). Strong 
               action engine card.

--- ULTIMATE ATTACK CARD ---

RANSOMWARE
  Cost: 6
  Effect: +3 Cards (Attack)
  Type: Heavy Attack Card
  Description: Heavy attack costing 6 money (expensive). Draws 3 cards for you. 
               Each other player chooses to either discard 2 cards from hand OR 
               gain a Bug. COUNTERED by Monitoring. Most powerful attack.

================================================================================
                            GAME PHASES
================================================================================

1. ACTION PHASE
   - Play action cards to get effects (draw cards, generate money, attack)
   - Limited by action count (usually 1 per turn, can increase with cards)

2. MONEY PHASE
   - Play money cards to generate spendable currency
   - Money accumulates (action card effects + money card plays)

3. BUY PHASE
   - Purchase cards from board using accumulated money
   - Limited by buy count (usually 1 per turn, can increase with cards)

4. CLEANUP PHASE
   - All played/unplayed cards go to discard pile
   - Discarded cards will be shuffled back into hand when deck empties
   - Bugs stay in deck as permanent penalties


================================================================================
                        STRATEGIC NOTES
================================================================================

BEGINNER STRATEGY (BigMoney):
- Focus on money cards (BITCOIN → ETHEREUM → DOGECOIN)
- Buy victory cards (METHOD → MODULE → FRAMEWORK) only late game
- Simple and stable but not competitive

INTERMEDIATE STRATEGY (Strategy Player):
- Include action cards for card generation
- Balance action chains with money production
- More interactive but less optimized

================================================================================
                            GAME RESOURCES
================================================================================

Victory Points System:
- Victory cards grant points at game END (not during play)
- Victory cards provide no gameplay benefit - only final scoring
- Victory point value: BUG (-1), METHOD (+1), MODULE (+3), FRAMEWORK (+6)
- Game ends when supply runs out or player can't refill hand

Money System:
- No persistent "money pool" - money accumulates per turn from cards/effects
- Unspent money is lost at end of turn
- Money can only be used in BUY phase

Actions System:
- Players start with 1 action per turn
- Action cards can grant more actions
- Unused actions are lost at end of ACTION phase

Buys System:
- Players start with 1 buy per turn  
- Some cards grant extra buys (SPRINT_PLANNING, DEPLOYMENT_PIPELINE, DAILY_SCRUM)
- Unused buys are lost at end of BUY phase
